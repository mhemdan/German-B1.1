15-Minute Daily Plan

  1. Monday: Grammar Focus (15 min)
  2. Study one grammar concept from GrammarProgression.md
  3. Review 2-3 example sentences

  4. Try creating 1 new example

  5. Tuesday: Vocabulary Focus (15 min)

  6. Learn 5-7 new words from VocabularyThemes.md
  7. Group them by theme

  8. Create quick associations

  9. Wednesday: Communication Focus (15 min)

  10. Practice 3-5 communication phrases
  11. Create a mini-dialogue using them

  12. Focus on pronunciation

  13. Thursday: Review Day (15 min)

  14. Review Monday's grammar concept
  15. Review Tuesday's vocabulary

  16. Review Wednesday's phrases

  17. Friday: Practice Exercises (15 min)

  18. Complete 1-2 exercises from memorization files
  19. Check your answers

  20. Note any difficulties

  21. Weekend: Real-world Application (15 min)

  22. Try using what you've learned in a real context
  23. Write a short paragraph using the week's material
  24. Record yourself speaking for 1-2 minutes

Weekend Intensive Plan

  2. Study one major grammar concept in depth
  3. Work through all examples in Grammar-Memorization files
  4. Create your own examples and practice exercises

  5. Review related concepts in GrammarProgression.md

  6. Saturday Afternoon: Vocabulary Building (2 hours)

  7. Focus on one thematic group from VocabularyThemes.md
  8. Learn 20-30 words with examples and context
  9. Create flashcards or mind maps

  10. Practice using words in sentences

  11. Sunday Morning: Communication Practice (2 hours)

  12. Study communication phrases from one Lektion
  13. Create dialogues using these phrases
  14. Practice speaking aloud or with a partner

  15. Role-play different scenarios

  16. Sunday Afternoon: Review & Integration (2 hours)

  17. Connect grammar concepts with vocabulary
  18. Create sentences using both
  19. Review everything learned over the weekend

  3. Day 4: Second review (3 days after initial learning)
  4. Day 7: Third review (1 week after initial learning)
  5. Day 14: Fourth review (2 weeks after initial learning)
  6. Day 30: Fifth review (1 month after initial learning)
  7. Day 90: Sixth review (3 months after initial learning)
